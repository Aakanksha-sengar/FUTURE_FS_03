Our Story

A Neighbourhood
Tradition

Tucked on BSNL Road in the heart of Sector 4, Hiran Magri, Pooja Bakery has been feeding families, celebrating birthdays, and starting mornings right for over five decades.

"Every item is made fresh in our kitchen — no shortcuts, just good ingredients and a lot of love."

Founded by Jagdish ji, we remain a family-run bakery with one simple promise: honest baking, every single day. That promise has never changed.
